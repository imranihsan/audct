Department Mission

The Department of Creative Technologies is committed to providing innovative education and research in artificial intelligence and emerging technologies to address global and societal challenges. The department fosters intellectual growth, ethical leadership, creativity, and entrepreneurship, preparing graduates to develop responsible technological solutions. Through collaboration with industry, academia, and government, the department contributes to technological and social development while promoting diversity, inclusion, and lifelong learning.

BSAI Program Objectives

PO - 01

Apply AI, Machine Learning, Intelligent Systems, and Automation Techniques to develop Innovative Solutions for Complex Real-World Problems.

PO - 02

Demonstrate Leadership, Collaboration, and Technical Competence in designing and managing AI-driven Systems within Multidisciplinary Environments.

PO - 03

Practice Artificial Intelligence with Ethical Responsibility and Continuously adapt to emerging AI Technologies through Lifelong Learning and Research.

BSDS Program Objectives

PO - 01

Apply Principles of Data Science, Analytics, Statistics, and Data Engineering to support Data-Driven Decision-Making across Diverse Domains.

PO - 02

Demonstrate Effective Communication, Collaboration, and Analytical Skills in managing Data-Centric Projects and Multidisciplinary Teams.

PO - 03

Uphold Ethical and Professional Standards in Data Analysis and Visualization while pursuing Lifelong Learning and Adapting to Evolving Technologies and Industry Practices.

BSSE Program Objectives

PO - 01

Apply Advanced Software Engineering Knowledge and Emerging Technologies to Develop Secure, Scalable, and Innovative Software Solutions for Real-World Challenges.

PO - 02

Demonstrate Leadership, Teamwork, Communication, and Project Management Skills in Multidisciplinary and Agile Software Development Environments.

PO - 03

Practice Software Engineering with Ethical Responsibility and Engage in Continuous Professional Development to Address Evolving Technological and Societal needs.
